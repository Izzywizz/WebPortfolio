Merry Chirstmas!!! An app insipired by snapchat filters that uses Augmented Reality teachonolgy similiar to that of Pokemon Go, using Vuforia well known AR tool, I was able to train the camera to reconise a certain pattern (you guess it, a christmas tree!), the user would then be greated with the familiar sights of christmas; a cheering santa, rudolf glowing nose and an animation of snowing fallling from the sky. A difficult but sophisticated delovepment with a design that focused on the joy of taking a pictire.
